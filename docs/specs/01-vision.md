# Vision

## Purpose

Modernize the Cook’s Camps website while preserving:

- The original content (lightly tightened only where helpful)
- The human voice and old-school spirit
- The historical / place-forward tone
- The simplicity of “how things work”

Primary visible upgrades:

- New, high-quality photos (especially interiors now; exteriors later)
- Improved readability and mobile support
- Faster performance and more consistent layout
- Easier maintenance with Markdown content files

## Audience

Typical guests include:

- Returning visitors who recognize the legacy site structure and tone
- New visitors who need modern readability, clear photos, and clear “how to book/request dates”

## North Star

**Heritage brochure with modern scaffolding**:

- calm, coastal, text-forward
- minimal “app UI”
- curated photography
- clear and friendly calls to action (call/email/request dates)

## Non-goals

To protect the spirit and keep scope sane:

- No booking engine / checkout / payments
- No aggressive “Book Now” funneling
- No resort-like branding or overly slick aesthetic
- No heavy animations, parallax, autoplay video, or large JS bundles
- No CMS initially (content edited via Markdown)

## Success criteria

A successful rebuild should:

- Feel instantly “Cook’s” to returning guests
- Be easy to browse on a phone
- Make it easy to choose a cottage and request dates
- Load quickly, especially photo pages
- Be simple for the owner to update via GitHub Markdown
