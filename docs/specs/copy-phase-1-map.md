# Phase 1 Copy Map

This phase covers the home page and shared layout microcopy.

## Source Rules

- Use historic site language where it is strong and still accurate.
- Use current site facts for operational details.
- Remove any copy that talks about the redesign itself.
- Treat repeated property facts as a defect.

## Copy Ownership

- Hero:
  - Carries the strongest identity statement.
  - This is the only place where the family continuity line belongs.
- Right-hand summary panel:
  - Operational facts only.
  - Weekly rentals, Saturday to Saturday, and what is in each cottage.
- Featured cottages intro:
  - Only introduces browsing.
  - No history or property-description copy here.
- Bottom feature cards:
  - Each card should describe its destination only.
- Sidebar:
  - Minimal location context only.
  - Prefer less copy over repeated copy.
- Footer:
  - Plain contact utility only.

## Home Page

### Hero

- Current:
  - Repeats descriptive property copy without carrying the family/history line.
- Historic source:
  - Continuous operation by the same family for over 85 years.
  - "`Cook's by the Ocean` is the finest beachside cottage colony in the world."
  - "Cooks overlooks the Atlantic Ocean ... with fourteen cottages available for weekly rental."
  - "The cottages are spaced over several acres ... at the top of an 80 foot sand dune."
  - "The property is just south of LeCount Hollow Beach with access to the largest private beach on the outer banks of Cape Cod."
- Direction:
  - Put the strongest family/history line here.
  - Follow it with one short paragraph that establishes place.
  - Do not repeat these lines elsewhere on the page.

### Right-Hand Summary Panel

- Current:
  - Works structurally, but should stay strictly operational.
- Historic source:
  - "All of our rentals are by the week, Saturday to Saturday."
  - "All cottages are complete with a full kitchen, half bath, outdoor shower, WiFi and Rinnai propane heater."
- Direction:
  - Keep the `At a Glance` heading.
  - Use direct factual copy only.
  - No scenic, historic, or emotional language.

### Featured Cottages Intro

- Current:
  - Still says more than it needs to.
- Historic/current source:
  - Large, medium, and studio grouping is still factual.
- Direction:
  - Reduce to a short browsing introduction.
  - Let the cottage cards do the rest.

### Bottom Feature Cards

- Current:
  - Broadly correct, but should stay destination-specific.
- Historic/current source:
  - Photo albums, historical photos, and simple contact flow all exist in legacy/current material.
- Direction:
  - Keep each card focused on where the link goes.
  - Avoid repeating home-page identity lines.

## Shared Layout

### Sidebar Intro Line

- Current:
  - Repeats the main property description.
- Historic/current source:
  - South Wellfleet, Cape Cod.
- Direction:
  - Reduce to location only.
  - Do not repeat the hero or operational facts here.

### Footer

- Current:
  - Needs to stay purely practical.
- Current source:
  - Email is the only public contact path.
- Direction:
  - Make the footer plain and direct.
