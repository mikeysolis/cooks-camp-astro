Marconi

Marconi is the largest cottage and has two separate bedrooms including one in the tower. The experince of sleeping in the tower bedroom is one that guests rarely forget. Marconi sits in the middle of the camps with a large deck overlooking the center of the compound and wrapping around to the south providing great views. Marconi sleeps seven people and has a fireplace.
