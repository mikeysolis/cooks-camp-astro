Historic material calls Marconi the grandaddy of the cottages at Cook's, and it still reads that way. Marconi is the largest cottage on the property and has two separate bedrooms, including one in the tower.

The current site describes the experience of sleeping in the tower bedroom as one guests rarely forget. A large deck looks over the center of the camps and wraps toward the south, and the fireplace keeps the place grounded in cooler weather.
