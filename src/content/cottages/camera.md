Camera is the shortest walk to the beach. From the deck it looks south over the Cape Cod National Seashore and east over the Atlantic, and it sleeps two in a studio-style layout.

Historic material remembers Camera as the old dark room from the Glider Camp days. That older origin story gives the cottage a character that still feels distinct even in the current site copy.
