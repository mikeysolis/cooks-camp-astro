Big Guilda sits slightly back from the rest of the cottages and remains one of the most private spots in the camps. It has two bedrooms, sleeps five, and has a fireplace for cooler evenings by the ocean.

The historic material remembers Big Guilda as a cottage moved back from the bluff in the late 1930s. That older sense of endurance still fits the place, and the outdoor shower offers a glimpse of the ocean.
