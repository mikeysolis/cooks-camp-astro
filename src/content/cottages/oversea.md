Oversea is one of the medium cottages and sits closest to the main house and the brick oven. It has one bedroom, sleeps four, and opens to deck views that make the setting feel close at hand.

The historic site described Oversea in simpler terms, as a medium-sized cottage with one bedroom and a bed living room. The present copy adds the deck and the nearness to the center of things, and both belong.
