Cook's sits above LeCount Hollow Beach in South Wellfleet, with the Atlantic stretching out beyond the bluff and the dune paths dropping toward the water.

From Route 6 in Wellfleet, the historic directions turn onto LeCount Hollow Road just past the information center in South Wellfleet and continue up the hill from there. The old route-style wording has been kept in mind, even while the new site presents it more cleanly.

The point of this page is not just navigation. It is to give visitors a sense of where the cottages actually sit: above the beach, close to the dunes, and shaped by the Atlantic on one side and the woods on the other.
