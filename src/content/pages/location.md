---
title: "Directions"
description: "Directions to Cook's by the Ocean in South Wellfleet."
---

Cross over the Cape Cod Canal on the Sagamore Bridge.

This will put you on the Mid Cape Highway. (Route 6)

Stay on Rt. 6 and you will come to a rotary in Orleans.

Go around the rotary and continue on Rt. 6 towards Provincetown.

Stay on Rt. 6 and you will come to the Wellfleet Drive In Theater on the left at the Wellfleet town line. Continue 2.7 miles past the drive in theater and turn right on LeCount Hollow Road, just past the information center in South Wellfleet.

Go down LeCount Hollow approx. 2/3 mile and you will see a post on the right with a little white whale saying Cook's and the number 420. It is the only paved right on LeCount Hollow excluding the bike trail parking lot.

Go up the drive and we are at the top of the hill.
