To find out about cottage availability, or answer any questions you may have, use the request form or send us an email at cooksbytheocean@gmail.com.

The most useful details to include are the cottage you have in mind, your preferred dates, and anything else that would help narrow the search.

All rentals are by the week, Saturday to Saturday.
