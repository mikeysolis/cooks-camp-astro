title: "Contact & Request Dates"
description: "Email, mailing address, and a simple path to request dates."
---

Contact remains intentionally simple. The current site uses email as the primary contact path, and the new site keeps that same approach.

If you are reaching out about availability, the most useful details to include are the cottage you have in mind, your preferred dates, and anything else that would help narrow the search.

No phone number is published in the rebuild, and weekly rentals remain Saturday to Saturday.
